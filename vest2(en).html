Rerun of the Short films in Competition and the Cinéfondation Selection in Paris

The short films in Competition in 2018 and the Cinéfondation Selection will be screened in Paris at the Cinéma du Panthéon and the Cinémathèque française.

Reprise OF THE FESTIVAL SHORT FILMS AT THE  Cinéma du Panthéon  AT 6PM ON 22 maY

6:00 pm: The winning films from the Cinéfondation Selection


8:00 pm: Short films. Official selection
In the presence of the directors and/or members of the Jury

 

► More information on the short films in Competition

► More information on the films of the Cinéfondation Selection


Cinéma du Panthéon
13 rue Victor Cousin
75005 Paris

 

THE 2018 Cinéfondation Séléction AT THE Cinémathèque française 11 JUNE, 9:00 PM

The Cinéfondation Selection is part of the Official Sélection of the Festival de Cannes. Created in 1998 in a bid to seek out new talents, the Cinéfondation selects 15-20 short films and mid-length films each year presented in Cannes by film schools from throughout the world.

On 11 June at 9:00 pm, the Cinémathèque française will show the following films:

COSÌ IN TERRA by Pier Lorenzo PISANO (Italy)
DONG WU XIONG MENG by DI Shen (China)
FRAGMENT DE DRAME by Laura GARCIA (France)
EL VERANO DEL LEÓN ELÉCTRICO by Diego CÉSPEDES (Chile)
INANIMATE  by  Lucia BULGHERONI (United Kingdom)


Cinémathèque Française
51 rue de Bercy
75012 Paris
